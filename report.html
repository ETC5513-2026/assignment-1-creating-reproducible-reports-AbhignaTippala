Customer Marketing Spend and Purchase Dataset

Author

Abhigna Tippala

INTRODUCTION: This dataset captures how customers behave through marketing spends, visits, and purchase outcomes across various segments, highlighting the value of customer insights for strategic decisions. This research aims to determine whether investing in marketing actually increases overall revenue and customer engagement. By analyzing customer segments such as Consumer, SMB, and Enterprise, we can identify which segment performs best and delivers the highest ROI. This will help make marketing strategies more effective.

RESEARCH: In this research, we will examine whether high marketing spending leads to increased purchase amounts and stronger customer engagement. We will then examine how this relationship will vary across the customer segments, including Consumer, SMB, and Enterprise. The aim is to understand which customer segment delivers the highest ROI.

DATA INTRODUCTION: The dataset contains information about customers, including customer ID, sign-up date, marketing spend, visits, purchase amount, segment, and notes. This data helps us understand consumer behavior and how marketing investments impact revenue and engagement. The data was cleaned by removing missing values from the purchase amount field. The dataset has been checked for inconsistencies to ensure that the data is clean and reliable.
